Past Projects

I previously worked for Insight Optics in Atlanta. In this role, I:

  • worked on adapting panorama creation and other computer vision algorithms for medical image processing,
  • developing tools for dataset creation and the continuous integration of new data into our models,
  • and designing a procedure for patient retinal imaging exams using a smartphone-based device.

I also previously worked in the Ethier lab at Georgia Tech. In this role, I:

  • created datasets and deep learning models used to quantify optic nerve damage in experimental models of glaucoma,
  • created easy-to-use tools for non computer-savvy biologists to process image data, including an imageJ plugin for the above,
  • and contributed to software used for digital image correlation, an imaging-based method for mechanical strain analysis.
