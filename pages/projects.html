Past Projects

I previously worked as an R&D engineer with Expanse Medical, a medical device start-up+incubator in Pleasanton, CA. In this role, I:

  • developed low-cost versions of existing interventional cardiology devices,
  • and invented new technologies in the interventional cardiology, neuro, and electrophysiology spaces.

I also previously worked for Insight Optics in Atlanta. In this role, I:

  • adapted panorama creation and other computer vision algorithms for medical image processing,
  • develped tools for dataset creation and the continuous integration of new data,
  • and designed procedure for retinal imaging exams using a smartphone.

I also previously worked in the Ethier lab at Georgia Tech. In this role, I:

  • created datasets and deep learning models used to quantify optic nerve damage in experimental models of glaucoma,
  • created easy-to-use tools for non computer-savvy biologists to process image data, including an imageJ plugin for the above,
  • and contributed to software used for digital image correlation, an imaging-based method for mechanical strain analysis.
